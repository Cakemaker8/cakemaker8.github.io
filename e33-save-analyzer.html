Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 Save Analyzer

This is a save file analyzer for the game Clair Obscur: Expedition 33. Currently being worked on.

You can find your save file in Users/AppData/Local/Sandfall/Saves; it will be named EXPEDITION#.sav, where # is the save slot that save is in (it can be 0-3). Once uploaded, tables will appear, and will be filled out based on what has been acquired.

Ideally, each item will also have a link to video on where it's at. That requires effort and will be a good minute. I would also like to add in the ability to see weapon and pictos level, pictos mastery, and maybe items (like tints). However, I am not sure if I will do that, since you can see that in game, and does not really require the wiki.

Disclaimer: I do not have the deluxe edition, and therefore cannot guarantee that those items are listed properly. There may also be missing items and issues with the current items; I am working to make sure everything is correct, but issues may persist.
